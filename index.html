About Me

I am an engineer at Kuaishou Inc., working on multimodal large language models, agentic systems, and recommendation. I received my M.Eng. from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2026, where I was honored as an Outstanding Graduate and received the National Scholarship.

Prior to that, I obtained my B.Eng. from Hefei University of Technology (HFUT) in 2023, graduating ranked #1 in my major and receiving the National Scholarship for Undergraduates.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Agentic Reinforcement Learning, Multimodal LLMs, Multimedia Understanding, and Recommendation Systems.
